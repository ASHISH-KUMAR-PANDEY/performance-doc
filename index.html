Stage VIP Club – Tambola
  • Owned end-to-end implementation of weekly Tambola within the Stage VIP Club Programme
  • Handled both product definition and engineering execution using AI
  • Open to all eligible users; promoted via push notifications, in-app messaging, reminder IVRs and home screen banners
  • Tracked cohort behavior week-over-week post each event

  • 75% WoW retention in Week 1 vs. 60% baseline

  • Cohort consumption lifted from 37% → 50% → 76%
  • 40% of players returned the following Sunday for repeat play
